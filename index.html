Production Service

We produce wherever the story takes us.

ONES operates from Warsaw, Bangkok and Barcelona — delivering full service production and line producing across Europe and South East Asia. From complex location shoots to large-scale studio builds, we bring international standards to every project, in every region.

Location Scouting

From metropolitan grids to volcanic shores — our location managers open access to landscapes few crews have ever filmed.

Casting

Local and international talent. Street, model, lifestyle and character casting handled in-house across three regions.

Budgeting & Planning

Transparent budgets, clear schedules and creative production planning that protect the idea on every shoot day.

Challenging Locations

Permits, logistics, safety and remote-shoot expertise — from Tatra peaks to tropical coasts and Iberian deserts.

Set Construction

Complex builds, art departments and SFX coordinated with senior crews who deliver at network broadcast standards.

Line Producing

Full line production across Poland, Thailand and Spain. One producer, one currency, one accountable team.
