What's included

Full service photo production.

Casting

Models, talent and street casting through trusted local agencies.

Authority liaison, customs and travel handled by senior producers.

Crew & Equipment

Photographers, assistants, stylists, HMU and full lighting packages.

Studio & Sets

Studio bookings, set construction, art direction and on-location builds.

Post Production

Retouching, colour grading and final delivery handled in-house.
